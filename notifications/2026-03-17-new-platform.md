We're excited to announce the **completely redesigned Conzent platform** — rebuilt from the ground up to make consent management faster, simpler, and more powerful.

## A Dashboard That Works for You

The new **dashboard** gives you a clear, real-time overview of your consent performance at a glance. Track acceptance rates, pageview trends, and compliance scores — all from a single, clean interface. No more digging through menus to find what matters.

## Banner Management, Simplified

Managing your consent banners has never been easier. The new **banner editor** lets you:

- **Configure layouts** — choose from multiple banner types and positions with a live preview
- **Customise colours** — fine-tune every element with light and dark theme support
- **Edit content** — update button labels, descriptions, and translations in one place
- **Set targeting rules** — control geo-targeting, consent expiration, and reload behaviour

Every change is reflected instantly, so you can see exactly what your visitors will see before you publish.

## What Else Is New

- **Streamlined site management** — add and switch between websites effortlessly
- **Improved cookie categorisation** — better detection and a cleaner overview of your cookies
- **Faster consent reporting** — filter, explore, and export your consent logs with ease
- **Modern, responsive design** — works beautifully on any screen size

We'll continue rolling out improvements over the coming weeks. If you have feedback or questions, don't hesitate to reach out.

Happy managing!

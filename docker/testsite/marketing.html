Facebook Pixel marketing

The Facebook/Meta Pixel sets these cookies:

  • _fbp — Facebook browser ID (90 days)
  • _fbc — Facebook click ID (90 days)
  • fr — Facebook ad tracking (90 days, on .facebook.com)

Facebook Pixel would load here (requires valid Pixel ID)

Google Ads / DoubleClick marketing

Google Ads conversion tracking sets:

  • _gcl_au — Google Ads conversion linker (90 days)
  • _gcl_aw — Google Ads click info (90 days)
  • IDE — DoubleClick ad targeting (1 year, on .doubleclick.net)
  • test_cookie — DoubleClick cookie test (15 min)

Google Ads tag would load here (requires valid conversion ID)

LinkedIn Insight Tag marketing

LinkedIn Insight sets:

  • _li_ss — LinkedIn ad analytics
  • li_sugr — LinkedIn browser ID (90 days)
  • bcookie — LinkedIn browser cookie (1 year)
